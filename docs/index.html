What is Bar Buddy?

Bar Buddy is your way to find the perfect setting for your night out. Whether you want a chill night to relieve some stress or go let loose and dance, Bar Buddy can help you find the perfect bar.

Find Deals

Whether you're a broke college student looking to have a good time for cheap, a local checking if their favorite spot has a special, or someone just passing through, Bar Buddy is here for you!

Covers and Wait Times

Want to know how much you have to pay before you get to the front of the line? Bar Buddy provides live data for wait times and covers at your favorite bars!

Add Your Bar

Are you an owner of a bar and want to show off your specials this week? Register your bar with Bar Buddy and update your customers with what's going on!
