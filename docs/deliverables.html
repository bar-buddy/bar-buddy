The History of Bar Buddy

  • Sales Pitch January 24, 2019

    Bar Buddy was hatched as a simple design project in the Fall of 2018 by team members Bryan Pauken and Noah Gleason for another class at The University of Alabama. For our sales pitch, we focused on college students to think about what we wanted in our favorite bar app.

  • Sprint 1 Presentation February 21, 2019

    One month later, Bar Buddy has an authentication system for customers to sign in and a design scheme that would light the fire for the development progress in the next sprint.

  • Sprint 2 Presentation April 4, 2019

    In the second sprint, Bar Buddy evolved into a fully functional application with local bars and their specials on the home page. Customers can now keep up with their favorite bars and their events or specials. Bar Buddy also allows managers to log in and update information about their bar, and add events and specials.
